Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

RemotePrompt ("the App") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

The App collects the following information:

  • Device Token: We collect Apple Push Notification Service (APNs) device tokens to send push notifications
  • Server Connection Information: Server URL, port number, and API key configured by the user
  • Certificate Fingerprint: Certificate information for server authentication

2. Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect:

  • Personally identifiable information such as name, email address, or phone number
  • Location data
  • Personal data on your device such as contacts, photos, or calendar
  • Content of prompts you enter (sent to server but not collected or stored by the developer)

3. Where Information Is Stored

  • iOS App: Configuration information is stored securely on your device
  • Server: Data is stored on servers managed and operated by users themselves. The developer has no access to user servers

4. Information Sharing

We do not share collected information with third parties except in the following cases:

  • Apple Push Notification Service: Minimum information required to send push notifications
  • Legal Requirements: When disclosure is required by law

5. Data Deletion

Users can delete their data in the following ways:

  • Uninstalling the iOS app will delete all data stored on the device
  • Server data can be deleted directly by users since they manage their own servers

6. Security

The App implements the following security measures:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for communication protection
  • Certificate pinning for server authentication
  • API key-based access control

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as necessary. We will notify you of any significant changes through the app.
